Mini GS Firmware Flasher

by Genex Space

Instructions:

  1. Connect your Mini GS (ESP32) to your computer via USB.
  2. Click "Connect to ESP32" below and select the serial port for your device.
  3. For Flashing:
    • Ensure the ESP32 is in Bootloader/Flashing mode (hold BOOT, press/release RESET, release BOOT).
    • The flasher will attempt an auto-reset; manual mode is a reliable backup.
    • Select a firmware and click its "Flash" button.
  4. For Serial Monitor:
    • Ensure your ESP32 is running application firmware that outputs serial data.
    • Set the correct baud rate and click "Start Monitor".
