Mini GS Firmware Flasher

Website by Genex Space

Instructions:

  1. Connect your Mini GS (ESP32) to your computer via USB.
  2. Put your ESP32 into Bootloader/Flashing mode:
    • Hold down the BOOT/FLASH button (usually GPIO0).
    • While holding BOOT, press and release the RESET/EN button.
    • Release the BOOT/FLASH button.
    • (Alternatively, some boards auto-reset into bootloader mode when the flasher connects.)
  3. Click "Connect to ESP32" below and select the serial port for your device.
  4. Once connected, choose a firmware and click its "Flash" button.

Select Firmware to Flash:

Test LED Firmware: A simple LED blinking test.

LoRa Test Firmware: For testing LoRa communication.

TinyGS Main Firmware: The primary TinyGS application.
